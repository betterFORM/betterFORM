Drop Down Date input control

An input which binds to a Node of type 'date' with an additional apperance "bf:dropdowndate" is rendered as a three piece select1 date control. The value may also be be typed in directly but allowed values for the year can be constrained by a custom appearance attribute. Values exceeding the range will 'jump back' to the lowest or highest allowed value respectively.

Example

Date with years range from ten years in the past to current year Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting from 1970 to current year Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting from 1970 to 1980 Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting from 1980 to 1970 Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting 50 years in the past from current year, ending 50 years in the future from current year Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting 50 years in the future from current year, ending 50 years in the past from current year Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting 30 years in the future from current year, ending 20 years in the future from current year Date +30:+20: Enter you Date of birth Not valid Date with years range starting 40 years in the past from current year, ending 30 years in the past from current year Enter you Date of birth Not valid

XForms Markup


<xf:group id="main" appearance="full">
    <xf:input id="input1" ref="input1/value[1]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate">
        <xf:label>Date with years range from ten years in the past to current year</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input2" ref="input1/value[2]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'1970'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting from 1970 to current year</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input3" ref="input1/value[3]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'1970:1980'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting from 1970 to 1980</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input4" ref="input1/value[4]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'1980:1970'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting from 1980 to 1970</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input5" ref="input1/value[5]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'-50:+50'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting 50 years in the past from current year, ending 50 years in the future from current year</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input6" ref="input1/value[6]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'+50:-50'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting 50 years in the future from current year, ending 50 years in the past from current year</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input7" ref="input1/value[7]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'+30:+20'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting 30 years in the future from current year, ending 20 years in the future from current year</xf:label>
        <xf:label>Date +30:+20:</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>

    <xf:input id="input8" ref="input1/value[8]" appearance="bf:dropdowndate" data-bf-params="date:'-40:-30'">
        <xf:label>Date with years range starting 40 years in the past from current year, ending 30 years in the past from current year</xf:label>
        <xf:hint>Enter you Date of birth</xf:hint>
        <xf:alert>Not valid</xf:alert>
    </xf:input>
</xf:group>
